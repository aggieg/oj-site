Contribute

You can get involved in Oddjob in many ways...

Bug fixes and Enhancements

Bug fixes and enhancements are very welcome. Please post them to the patches tracker.

Write an Oddball

Extend the functionality of Oddjob by creating an Oddball. Oddballs are plugins for Oddjob and writing them is really simple. See the Oddballs section of the developer guide for more information on writing an Oddball.

Spread the Word

Has Oddjob worked for you? Has Oddjob saved you time? Why not use that time to tell the world about Oddjob by writing an article or blog. The more people that use Oddjob, the more robust and useful it will become!
